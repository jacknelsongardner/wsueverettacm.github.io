About CougHacks

CougHacks is free for all participants. You do not need to be an expert programmer to participate, just bring a desire to learn more about technology! Hardware will not be provided to participants, so bring a laptop. The event begins April 5th at 11:00 A.M. and ends on April 6th at 6:00 P.M. Participants are encouraged to stay for the entire event to submit their project for judging and be eligible for prizes. Teams can have up to 4 members. You can prearrange teams prior to the event or we can help teams form as projects begin. Sign up using the button above.
