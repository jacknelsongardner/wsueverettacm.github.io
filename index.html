About CougHacks

CougHacks is free for all participants. You do not need to be an expert programmer to participate, just bring a desire to learn more about technology! Hardware will not be provided to participants, so bring a laptop. The event begins March 13th at 11 am and continues through the 14th until 11 pm online. Participants are encouraged to stay for the entire event to submit their project for judging and be eligible for prizes. Teams can have up to 4 members. You can prearrange teams prior to the event or we can help teams form as projects begin. Sign up using the button above.

CougHacks FAQ

CougsHacks is a collegiate level hackathon held at WSU Everett.

CougsHacks will be hosted online over zoom and discord, after registrastion you will recieve a zoom and discord link.

All students attending any University, College, or High School can attend.

CougHacks is completely free to attend.

All students must bring a laptop and a charger.

Team sizes are 4 people maximum.

During the start of the event we will host a team forming activity to ensure that all attendies have a team.
